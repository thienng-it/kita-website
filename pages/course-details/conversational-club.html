Conversational Club

The best way to improve your speaking is to practice! Our Conversational Club provides a relaxed, friendly, and supportive setting where you can chat about various topics with a native speaker and fellow learners. It's the perfect way to build fluency and overcome the fear of speaking.

Club Details

  • Format: Weekly Group Discussion
  • When: Every Friday, 7 PM - 8 PM
  • Moderator: Native English Speaker
  • Fee: $50 / month

Why You Should Join

  • Practice speaking in a no-pressure environment.
  • Learn new idioms, expressions, and vocabulary naturally.
  • Improve your listening skills and pronunciation.
  • Make new friends and have fun while learning.
