Guitar for Beginners

Ever dreamed of playing your favorite songs on the guitar? Our beginner's group class is the perfect place to start! Learn in a fun, supportive environment with fellow enthusiasts and start your journey to becoming a confident guitar player.

Course Details

  • Lesson Type: Group
  • Duration: 4 sessions / month
  • Instructor: David Chen
  • Fee: $120 / month

What You'll Learn

  • The parts of the guitar and how to hold and tune it.
  • Essential chords and how to switch between them smoothly.
  • Basic strumming patterns for different rhythms.
  • How to play simple and popular songs.
